# __Login, Dashboard, and Registration application__ :computer: 
* A simple login form where users will login or register to navigate dashboard of list of students.<br/>
## __Registration page__
* Lets users input their information if the user has no account.<br/>
## __Login page__
* Users input their account details to login.<br/>
## __Dashboard page__
* Users will be brought to the Dashboard where the Students data can be seen.<br/>
## __To use the application__ :sparkles:

You will first be brought to the login page, where you will input your account details such the username and the password, then press the login button to go to the dashboard page. If you don't have account yet, you need to click the link "No account yet? Click here to register", to create your account in the registration page.

In the registration page, input your account information, such as your Name, Username, Email, Password, and the Confirmed password. Then press register to create your account. If you already have an account you can click "Already have an account? Login here" to go to the login page.

Lastly, if you successfully login, you will be brought to the dashbaord page where you can now check the lists of students.
